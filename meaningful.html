Meaningful activities

How do you make sure that the person can have meaningful activities today?

WHY IS THIS IMPORTANT?

By suggesting meaningful activities, you can promote the persons Sence of Cohesence (SOC). When you listen to the persons feelings and mood today, you will be able to take into account the elderlys daily resourses and thereby provide activities that are suitable and feels important for the person.

Note!
For training this topic the student/participant must already posses the following key skills and basic knowledge about:
  • body ageing processes
  • theory of person-centered care
  • the Salutogenesis model
  • the positive health model or similar framework highlighting the holistic connection between physical, mental and social health
  • how to guide daily functioning with a resource-oriented approach
  • how to promote a meaningful life by using methods that increase participation

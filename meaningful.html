How do you make sure that the person can have meaningful activities today?

WHY IS THIS IMPORTANT?

By suggesting meaningful activities, you can promote the persons Sence of Cohesence (SOC). When you listen to the persons feelings and mood today, you will be able to take into account the elderlys daily resourses and thereby provide activities that are suitable and feels important for the person.
